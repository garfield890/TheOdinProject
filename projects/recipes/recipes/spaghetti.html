Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin, cylindrical pasta that is a staple of Italian cuisine and loved worldwide. Made primarily from wheat flour and water, it is versatile and pairs well with a variety of sauces, from classic marinara and rich Bolognese to creamy Alfredo or light olive oil-based dressings. Spaghetti is traditionally cooked al dente, giving it a slightly firm texture that enhances its ability to hold sauces and flavors. Often served with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs, it is a comforting and satisfying dish that can be enjoyed in simple preparations or elevated with gourmet ingredients like seafood, truffles, or high-quality meats. Whether served as part of a casual meal or a fine dining experience, spaghetti remains a timeless favorite.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. Prepare the sauce base: Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes: Pour in crushed tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Combine: Toss the cooked spaghetti into the sauce, mixing well to coat evenly.
  5. Serve: Plate the spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese. Enjoy!
